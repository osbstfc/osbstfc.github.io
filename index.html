Obsidian Star Bot (OSB) is a free to use bot for Star Trek Fleet Command

OSB is a free non-invasive, bot for the desktop version (Windows PC) of the online game Star Trek Fleet Command (STFC BOT).

Why use a bot?

Three words: more free time.

Spending hours in front of a screen daily will eventually take a toll on your real life. Say goodbye to the endless grind that comes with the pursuit of specific loot, ship parts, faction reputation, resources etc. and start enjoying the game without having to manually do all the tidious work required by the game every day.

This free STFC BOT is not meant to replace actually playing the game (although it could).

This bot meant to get you a good night's sleep and more free time to spend with your family and friends without any worry that you miss the completion of an event, or that your miners get killed, or that your base is being raided, or... or...

Why use this bot?

Plain and simple: it's the best STFC bot out there. And the bot is free to use. But don't take my word for it... try it.

No matter how many docks you have, no matter what ships and crews you have, no matter what type of mining you need to do, no matter what type of hostiles you need to kill, no matter what type of event you want to complete, this bot got you covered.
All at the same time, the bot will mine any type of resource in any system, will change multiple ships and crews and will go to attack any type of hostile you set (both in regular or deep space systems). It will use EXOCOMPS, ship special abilities, hailing frequencies, will send alliance help, will collect chests, dailies, event rewards, will convert tokens and collect items in the refinery and ... so much more!

All of this with granular settings control, adapting the bot to whatever game style you play.

OSB STFC BOT is free to use and it does NOT require to register an user account (no email or any type of personal data).
